Audio as a research interface

Turning literature review into a repeatable listening habit.

Draft placeholder. This page will develop the idea behind Audio Epilepsy Digest: audio as a lightweight interface for recurring research attention.

Reading is still the source of truth. But audio can create a different kind of contact with the literature: repeated exposure, easier re-entry, and a way to keep a research question active during otherwise unused time.

The useful version is not a replacement for source review. It is a structured habit around source-grounded summaries, transcript checks, and human judgment.
