What clinic notes are really for

Patients, physicians, and AI scribes.

Draft placeholder. This page exists so the writing section has a durable home while the essay is being written.

A clinic note is not only a billing artifact or a memory aid. It is a compact public surface of clinical reasoning: what mattered, what was uncertain, what changed, and what the next person should trust enough to act on.

AI scribes make this question more urgent. If notes become easier to generate, they also need stronger standards for source fidelity, patient readability, and clinician review.
